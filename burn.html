⚡ Universal Burn First Aid (Before Analysis)

1 Stop the burning process: Remove the person from the source of the burn.
2 Cool the burn: Run cool (not cold) water over the burn for at least 10–20 minutes.
3 Remove tight items: Remove rings or tight clothing before swelling occurs.
4 Do NOT: Do not apply ice, butter, toothpaste, or ointments.
5 Call 108 if: The burn is large, deep, on the face/hands/groin, or caused by chemicals/electricity.
