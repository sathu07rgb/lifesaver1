Assess the Situation

An unconscious person requires immediate assessment. Answer the critical question below so we can guide you through the correct procedure.

Is the person breathing?

⚡ Quick Reference — Recovery Position

1 Kneel beside the person.
2 Place the arm nearest you at a right angle to their body.
3 Bring the other arm across their chest, holding the back of their hand against their nearest cheek.
4 With your other hand, pull their far knee up so their foot is flat on the floor.
5 Pull the knee towards you to roll them onto their side, then tilt their head back slightly.
🚨 SOS Activated

This will alert all emergency services to your location. Only proceed in a real emergency.
