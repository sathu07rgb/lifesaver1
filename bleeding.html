First Aid Steps

1 Apply firm, direct pressure using a clean cloth or bandage.
2 Do NOT remove the cloth if it soaks through — add more on top.
3 Elevate the injured limb above heart level if possible.
4 If bleeding doesn't stop in 10 mins, call 108 immediately.
5 Watch for signs of shock: pale skin, rapid breathing, dizziness.
