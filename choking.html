⚡ Quick Reference — Heimlich Maneuver

1 Ask "Are you choking?" — if they can't speak or cry, act immediately.
2 Give 5 firm back blows between the shoulder blades with the heel of your hand.
3 Give 5 abdominal thrusts (Heimlich maneuver) — thrust upward and inward sharply.
4 Alternate 5 back blows and 5 abdominal thrusts until the object is removed.
5 If person becomes unconscious — start CPR and call 108 immediately.
